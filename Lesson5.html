Cybersecurity Roles

Incident Responders Governance, Risk, and Compliance Threat Intelligence Security Operations Offensive Security

What is an Incident Responder?

They detect and assess cyberthreats and incidents, along with conduct digital forensics.

What is GRC?

They work with other security teams within the organization to identify and/or monitor risks to a business.

What is Threat Intelligence?

They help an organization identify and mitigate potential threats.

What is Security Operations?

They design,implement, and maintain cyber security protocols.

What is Offensive Security?

They hack systems with permission in order to find vulnerabilities and problems.
