Why Is Cybersecurity Important?

Importance Of Cybersecurity? Effects of Cyberattacks

Importance

As our society continues investing more and more in technological advances, its important that our technology is secure and protected from attacks. In today's time, cyberattacks are extremely common. According to an IBM report, the average cost of a data breach is $9.44 Million!

Effects

  • Denial Of Service for many
  • Leak of user data
  • Attackers using the infiltrated website as a gateway to other networks
